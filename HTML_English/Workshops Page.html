Silver IT Fest

18 Mar 2017 - 20 Mar 2017

The Silver IT Fest IT classes offer seniors the opportunity to learn more IT skills and pick up new hobbies. Seniors can learn how to use a basic computer, send e-mails, check on health information, social networking, e-travel and many more!
Conducted in the four official languages, the IT classes have been a hit among seniors since it was launched 10 years ago. The classes are taught by seniors and volunteers from the Friends of Silver Infocomm (FSI), who will pace the class comfortably to suit the seniors’ learning needs.
