General questions regarding Smart Nation

What is Smart Nation?

Smart Nation is a national effort to co-create a future of better living for all through tech-enabled solutions. It aims to rally the collective efforts of people, businesses and goverment to work together to support better living, create more opportunities, and support stronger communities by harnessing info-comm technologies, network and big data. In simpler terms, Smart Nation aims to improve lives through technological advancements.

Why Smart Nation?

Smart Nation opens up new possibilties to enhance the way people work, live and play. Smart Nation also support better living, stronger communities and create more opportunities for all.

What can i look forward to?

Smart Nation aims to make a impact in 5 key domains.
  1. Transport
  2. Home & Environment
  3. Business productivity
  4. Health & enabled ageing
  5. Public sector services

Does Smart Nation equates to lesser job opportunities?

To a certain extent,yes. However, while some jobs disappear, there are new jobs and platforms that will emerge. As long as one possess the neccessary skills and tools,this would not be a concern.

Should we be worried about hackers as Smart Nation rises?

According to a recent UN study, Singapore has been ranked first among other countries. This means that the goverment will be stepping up their efforts to minimise the risk of hackers intruding. However, one should also be vigilant and take measures to prevent hackers from having opportunities for intrusion.

Does Smart Nation result in a loss of culture?

No, the culture can still be preserved. In fact, Smart Nation can help people to understand more about the culture.

Will the older generation be left behind as Smart Nation progresses?

This should not be a concern as governemnt will be stepping up their efforts to help elderly become more tech-savvy. After all, Smart Nation strives to improve the living for all.
