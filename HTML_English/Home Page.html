Welcome to Smart Nation website

This is an informational website whereby we aim to empower those around our seniors so that they can assist them as Singapore moves towards a Smart Nation. We also want to allow the seniors to understand, appreciate and embrace Smart Nation. After all, our seniors have worked hard their whole life and now it is time for them to enjoy. Thus with the advent of Smart Nation, we hope that when the elderly learns more about Smart Nation, they can use the technology to their advantage.
