Notice to Tutors: Personal Data Protection Act

In line with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), tutors are reminded to maintain confidentially of the tutees contact information. Also, when exporting requests, contact information will no longer be included.

Please refer to the PDPA on the Singapore Statutes Online here (You will redirected to Singapore Statutes Online, your session with Dashboard will not be affected).

Under the PDPA, the following activites are prohibtied:

  • Forward tutees contact information to telemarkerters/advertisting agencies
  • Use tutees contact information for personal activites without explict permission
  • Leave tutees contact information unsecured

Note that list is non exhaustive and subject to change

Failure to comply are grounds for the tutor to be dismissed and possibly charged under the PDPA
